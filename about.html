About PhD Shodh

Experienced guidance, ethical approach, measurable milestones

PhD Shodh is a team of academic mentors and research analysts dedicated to helping doctoral students across India navigate the thesis journey. Our approach is milestone-driven, transparent and tailored to university regulations and discipline-specific standards.

We are not a thesis-writing service; we provide mentoring, editing, data analysis, formatting and viva coaching. Students retain full academic responsibility and authorship of their work.
