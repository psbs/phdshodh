SPSS vs R for thesis analysis — pick the right tool

Oct 2025 • 8 min read

Both SPSS and R are popular — each has trade-offs. This post helps you choose the right one for your thesis based on reproducibility, learning curve and analysis needs.

When to choose SPSS

  • Simple GUI-driven workflows and quick descriptive tests
  • When supervisors prefer SPSS outputs

When to choose R

  • Advanced modelling, reproducible scripts, and custom visuals
  • Better for factor analysis, SEM (with lavaan), and reproducibility

Recommendation

If you need reproducibility and advanced analyses, choose R. For quicker GUI based tasks and limited analyses, SPSS may suffice. We can run analyses in either and provide write-ups compatible with thesis chapters.
