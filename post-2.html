Writing a strong literature review — practical steps

Sep 2025 • 6 min read

A literature review should synthesise and critique — not merely list sources. Below are practical steps to create a focused, citable review.

  1. Define search keywords and databases
  2. Screen for relevance and quality
  3. Organise by theme, not chronologically
  4. Critique gaps and build the rationale for your study

We also offer literature mapping as a separate service to help you build a strong, defendable review quickly.
