Viva preparation: checklist & mock questions

A concise checklist and sample questions to help you prepare and perform confidently in your viva voce.

Checklist

  • One-page summary of the thesis (objectives, methods, main findings)
  • 10–12 slide presentation covering background, methods, key results, and contribution
  • Printed copy of thesis and key tables/figures
  • Similarity report and ethical clearance documents (if applicable)
  • Mock viva sessions with peers/supervisor

Sample questions

  • What motivated this study and why is it important?
  • How did you select your sample and why is it appropriate?
  • Explain one key statistical method you used and why.
  • What are the limitations and how would you address them in future work?
  • What is the original contribution of your thesis?
