Preparing for viva — most asked questions and templates

Aug 2025 • 7 min read

We run mock vivas and provide a checklist for slides and common Q&A. Focus on clarity: explain your methods, justify choices and highlight contributions.

Common examiner questions

  • Why did you choose this method?
  • How do your results address the research questions?
  • What are the study limitations and future directions?

We provide model answers and run timed mock sessions to build confidence.
