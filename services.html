Frequently Asked Questions

Do you guarantee publication?

We guarantee high-quality, submission-ready manuscripts. However, acceptance depends on the journal's peer review process. We support you through revisions until acceptance.

Can you follow my university's specific format?

Yes — we strictly adhere to your university's thesis template and submission guidelines. Please share your handbook during the consultation.

Is the work confidential?

Absolutely. We sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) on request. Your data and research ideas remain your intellectual property.

How do payments work?

We work on a milestone basis. You pay for each chapter or stage as it is delivered and approved. This ensures trust and quality control.
